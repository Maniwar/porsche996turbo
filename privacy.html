Privacy notice

Privacy & your information — 996 Turbo listing

Effective 11 July 2026 · Version 1.0

This is a private sale of a single car — a 2003 Porsche 911 Turbo — by its individual owner. The chat assistant (“the concierge”) on the listing answers your questions and, if you choose, passes your inquiry to the owner. This notice explains what personal information the concierge collects, why, who it is shared with, how long it is kept, and the privacy rights you have. It is written to be read, not to bury you in fine print.

The short version

  • You can browse and chat without giving your name. You only share contact details if you choose to make an offer or request a viewing.
  • Your chat messages are processed by an AI provider (Anthropic) to write replies. They are not used to train its AI models.
  • Your information is used only for this car sale. It is never sold, rented, or used for advertising.
  • It is handled by a few service providers (Supabase, Anthropic, Resend, GitHub) and, if you play the video, YouTube. Data is processed in the United States.
  • You can ask what is held about you, get a copy, correct it, or have it deleted — email mberenji@gmail.com.

4. The AI concierge

The concierge is powered by an AI model provided by Anthropic. When you chat, your messages are sent to Anthropic’s systems to generate a reply, then returned to you. Two things worth knowing:

  • Under Anthropic’s commercial terms, your chat content is used only to produce the response — it is not used to train or improve their AI models.
  • The AI does not make any decision that has a legal or similarly significant effect on you. It answers questions and can hand your inquiry to the owner; a human — the owner — makes all actual decisions about the sale.

5. Who we share it with

We don’t sell, rent, or trade your information, and we don’t use it for advertising or cross-context behavioral tracking. We share it only with the service providers that make the listing work, each acting on our instructions:

Supabase — hosts the concierge’s backend and stores conversations, inquiries, and sign-in records. Database, authentication & server hosting (USA).
Anthropic — the AI that reads your messages and writes replies (see §4). AI model provider (USA).
Resend — delivers your inquiry to the owner by email when you submit a form. Transactional email provider (USA).
GitHub Pages — serves the listing web page. Static web hosting / CDN (USA).
YouTube (Google) — only if you play the cold-start video. The clip is embedded in privacy-enhanced mode, but pressing play connects your browser to Google/YouTube, which then receives your request and IP address under Google’s own privacy policy. Third-party video (loads only on play).

We may also disclose information if required by law, or to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims (for example, a dispute arising from the sale).

8. How long we keep it

We keep your information only as long as it’s needed for this sale:

  • Conversations and usage data are cleared on a routine schedule.
  • Inquiries are kept while the owner is in touch with you and while the car is for sale, then removed after the sale concludes.
  • We keep the minimum needed to meet any legal obligation or resolve a dispute, and no longer.

You can ask us to delete your information sooner (see §10).

9. How we protect it

Information is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored with providers that encrypt data at rest and restrict access. Access to inquiries is limited to the owner. No method of transmission or storage is ever completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

10. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Know / access what personal information we hold about you and get a copy;
  • Correct inaccurate information;
  • Delete your information;
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, and where technically possible, request portability of what you provided;
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this won’t affect what happened before).

To exercise any of these, email mberenji@gmail.com. We’ll respond within a reasonable time (and within any period the law requires), we won’t charge you or treat you differently for asking, and we may need to confirm your identity first.

If you are in California

We do not sell or “share” your personal information as those terms are used under California law, and we do not process it for cross-context behavioral advertising — so there is nothing to opt out of on that front. You still have the rights to know, delete, and correct described above, and the right not to be discriminated against for using them. We honor browser opt-out signals (such as Global Privacy Control) to the extent they apply.

If you are in the UK or EU/EEA

The legal bases we rely on are set out in §3. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority, though we’d appreciate the chance to resolve any concern with you first.

11. Cookies, storage & tracking signals

This listing does not use advertising or analytics tracking cookies. The concierge stores a small session key in your browser’s local storage so a conversation can continue; you can clear it any time by clearing your browser’s site data. The embedded video uses YouTube’s privacy-enhanced mode and loads only when you press play. Because we don’t sell data or track you across sites, “Do Not Track” and Global Privacy Control signals have no sale or cross-site tracking to stop.

12. Children

This listing is intended for adults buying a car. We don’t knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided information, email us and we’ll delete it.
